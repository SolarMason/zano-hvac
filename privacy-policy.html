Privacy Policy

Last updated · February 23, 2025

At ZANO HVAC/R & Plumbing, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to our website, mobile application, and any associated services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

You may provide personal information when you create an account, request service, subscribe to updates, or contact us. This may include: full name, email, phone, mailing/billing address, and payment info (processed by secure third parties).

1.2 Automatically Collected

  • IP address, browser/device info
  • Geographic location (if permitted)
  • Site usage data
  • App activity logs

1.3 Cookies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To process service requests and inquiries
  • To send appointment reminders and updates
  • To personalize your experience
  • To process payments securely
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share with service providers (payment processors, scheduling), to comply with legal requirements, or in business transfers.

4. Data Security

We use SSL encryption, secure servers, and limited internal access. No method of transmission is 100% secure — keep your credentials confidential.

5. Your Rights

  • Access & correction
  • Deletion (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-out of marketing
  • Restrict processing

To exercise these rights, email support@zanohvac.com.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use after updates means acceptance.
